Families have been enjoying the scenic beaches of Keansburg Amusement Park for over 100 years. Not only are our beaches FREE, but offer a beautiful summer breeze while overlooking the skyline of lower Manhattan. - Beaches have NO lifeguards. Swim at your own risk. -Beach fires and grilling are strictly prohibited. Police will be called and fines will assessed for violations. - Garbage cans are provided. Please help us to keep YOUR beach clean.
Keansburg Beach is a popular coastal destination located in Keansburg, a borough in Monmouth County, New Jersey, USA. Nestled along the shores of the Raritan Bay, this beach offers a scenic and relaxing escape for locals and visitors alike. With its close proximity to the New York City metropolitan area, Keansburg Beach provides a convenient retreat for those seeking a break from urban life.
The beach boasts a wide expanse of sandy shoreline, providing ample space for beachgoers to enjoy activities such as sunbathing, picnicking, and playing beach games. The gentle waves of the bay make it a suitable spot for swimming and wading, creating a family-friendly environment
